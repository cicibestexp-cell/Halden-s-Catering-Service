Terms and Conditions

1. Introduction

Welcome to Halden's Event Management & Catering. By accessing our platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully before using our services.

2. Services Provided

Halden's provides comprehensive event management and catering services, including but not limited to venue setup, food preparation, staff allocation, and scheduling. All services are subject to availability and prior booking confirmation.

3. Booking and Reservations

All bookings must be made through our official platform. A reservation is only considered confirmed once a deposit has been successfully processed and a confirmation email has been dispatched. We reserve the right to decline or cancel any reservation at our discretion, particularly in cases of suspected fraud or unavailability of resources.

4. Payment Terms

Clients are required to pay a non-refundable deposit to secure their booking. The remaining balance must be settled at least 7 days prior to the event date. We accept major credit cards and certified e-payment methods. Failure to complete payment may result in the cancellation of your event.

5. Cancellations and Refunds

Cancellations made more than 30 days before the event will receive a 50% refund of the deposit. Cancellations made within 30 days of the event are strictly non-refundable. In the unlikely event that Halden's must cancel your booking, a full refund will be provided.

6. Client Responsibilities

The client is responsible for ensuring that the venue is accessible to our team at the agreed-upon time. Any damages caused by the client or their guests to Halden's equipment will be billed directly to the client.

7. Liability

Halden's shall not be held liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of our services. Our total liability is limited to the amount paid for the specific service in question.

8. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these Terms and Conditions at any time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
